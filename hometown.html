KIM KWANG SEOK
STREET

How to go

Bangcheon Market in Daebong-dong, Jung-gu,
Daegu is the birthplace of Kim Gwang-seok, so there are many things related to him,
including the murals of Kim Gwang-seok, the streets of Kim Gwang-seok,
and the seats of Kim Gwang-seok. There are many cafes and shops around the street that look like the old days.
